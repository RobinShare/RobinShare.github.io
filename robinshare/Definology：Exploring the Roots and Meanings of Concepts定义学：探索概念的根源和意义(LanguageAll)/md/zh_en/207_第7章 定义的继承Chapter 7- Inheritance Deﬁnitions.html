Chapter 7: Inheritance Deﬁnitions

    定义的继承是指在一个概念被定义的过程中，通过引用已有的定义或概念，对其进行扩展或进一步解释，从而产生新的定义或概念。在定义的继承中，新定义或概念通常具有与被引用的定义或概念相似或相关的特征。

    Defined inheritance refers to the process of defining a concept by referencing existing definitions or concepts and expanding or further explaining them to produce new definitions or concepts. In defined inheritance, the new definition or concept usually has similar or related characteristics to the referenced definition or concept.

    定义的继承可以通过多种方式进行，如对已有定义或概念的部分或全部内容进行修改、扩展、解释等。此外，定义的继承也可以通过将已有定义或概念作为子概念，来构建更为复杂的概念体系。

    Defined inheritance can be achieved through various means, such as modifying, expanding, or explaining part or all of an existing definition or concept. In addition, defined inheritance can also construct more complex conceptual systems by treating existing definitions or concepts as sub-concepts.

    例如，在数学中，集合的定义可以通过继承包含它的更一般的概念——类的定义来实现。集合可以被看作是一种特殊的类，即其成员的特征可以被用来明确地区分它们与非成员的区别。类的定义比集合的定义更为一般，它可以包含集合之外的其他对象。因此，通过类的定义来继承集合的定义，可以使得集合的概念更加丰富和广泛。

    For example, in mathematics, the definition of a set can be achieved by inheriting the definition of the more general concept - class. A set can be seen as a special type of class, where the features of its members can be used to clearly distinguish them from non-members. The definition of a class is more general than that of a set, as it can include objects outside of sets. Therefore, by inheriting the definition of a class to define a set, the concept of a set can become richer and more extensive.

    为了便于理解作进一步解释，在数学中，集合是一个重要的概念，它是由若干个元素组成的整体。但是，在定义集合时，我们通常会引入更一般的概念——类（或称为类型），用来描述集合所属的更大的范畴。类是由若干个具有相同性质的元素组成的整体，而这些元素可以是集合、数字、函数等等。

    To further explain, in mathematics, a set is an important concept consisting of several elements. However, when defining a set, we usually introduce the more general concept - class (or type), to describe the larger category to which the set belongs. A class is a whole composed of several elements with the same properties, and these elements can be sets, numbers, functions, and so on.

    以集合的定义为例，我们可以定义一个集合A为由元素1、2、3组成的整体，即A={1,2,3}。但是，我们也可以定义一个类B，它包含所有只有三个元素的集合，即B={S | S是由三个元素组成的集合}。这样，集合A就可以继承自类B，因为A是由三个元素组成的集合之一，它符合类B的定义。

    For example, we can define a set A as a whole composed of elements 1, 2, and 3, that is A={1, 2, 3}. However, we can also define a class B that contains all sets with only three elements, that is B={S | S is a set composed of three elements}. In this case, set A can inherit from class B, as A is one of the sets composed of three elements, which meets the definition of class B.

    通过继承包含它的更一般的概念——类的定义，集合的定义可以更加简单和抽象，同时也能够与更多的数学概念进行连接和应用。这种方式可以避免重复定义相似的概念，提高数学理论的统一性和简洁性。

    By inheriting the definition of the more general concept - class, the definition of a set can become simpler and more abstract, while also being connected and applied to more mathematical concepts. This approach can avoid defining similar concepts repeatedly, and improve the unity and conciseness of mathematical theory.

    另一个例子是在计算机科学中，对象的概念可以通过对类的定义进行继承来扩展。对象是类的实例化，即根据类的定义创建的具体对象。因此，对类的定义进行修改或扩展，可以使得对象的概念更加具体和明确。例如，一个名为“人”的类可以被继承为名为“学生”的类，以表示“学生”是一种“人”的特殊情况。通过这种继承，可以使得“学生”这一概念具有“人”的所有特征，同时增加“学生”特有的属性和方法。

    Another example is in computer science, where the concept of an object can be extended by inheriting the definition of a class. An object is an instantiation of a class, that is, a concrete object created based on the definition of a class. Therefore, modifying or extending the definition of a class can make the concept of an object more specific and clear. For example, a class named "person" can be inherited as a class named "student", to indicate that "student" is a special case of "person". Through this inheritance, the concept of "student" can have all the characteristics of "person", while also adding specific properties and methods for "student".

What are the risks involved in the inheritance of definitions?

    1.死板地继承：定义的继承如果过于死板，就可能无法适应新的时代和新的发展。如果对定义的继承进行过度保护，会限制新思想和新理念的出现和发展，甚至阻碍整个学科或领域的创新和发展。

    1. Inheritance by rigidity: Inheritance of definitions that is too rigid may fail to adapt to new times and new developments. If the inheritance of definitions is overprotected, it can limit the emergence and development of new ideas and concepts, or even hinder the innovation and development of an entire discipline or field.

    2.概念模糊：定义的继承可能导致概念模糊。由于时间的推移和语言的变化，某些定义中的术语和概念可能已经过时或不再适用。如果不及时更新和调整这些定义，就会导致概念模糊，进而影响相关领域的发展。

    2. Conceptual ambiguity: Inheritance of definitions may lead to conceptual ambiguity. Due to the passage of time and changes in language, the terms and concepts in some definitions may be outdated or no longer applicable. Failure to update and adjust these definitions in a timely manner can lead to conceptual ambiguity, which in turn can affect the development of related fields.

    3.误导性：如果定义的继承过于依赖历史定义，就可能导致误导性。历史定义中可能存在错误或偏见，如果将其作为现代定义的基础，就可能误导人们对相关领域的理解和认识。

    3. Misleading: If the succession of definitions relies too much on historical definitions, it may lead to misleading. Errors or biases may exist in historical definitions, and if they are used as the basis for modern definitions, they may mislead people's understanding and awareness of the relevant fields.

    4.地域性：定义的继承可能存在地域性。不同地区和文化背景下，对某些概念和术语的理解和定义可能存在差异。如果将某些地区的定义过于强制性地应用到其他地区，就可能导致误解和不适用。

    4. Territoriality: There may be territoriality in the inheritance of definitions. There may be differences in the understanding and definition of certain concepts and terms in different regions and cultural contexts. If definitions from certain regions are applied too compulsorily to other regions, it may lead to misunderstanding and non-application.

    5.语言障碍：定义的继承也可能存在语言障碍。不同语言和文化背景下，对某些概念和术语的理解和定义可能存在差异。如果没有充分考虑语言差异，就可能导致理解和沟通上的障碍。

    5. Language barriers: There may also be language barriers to the succession of definitions. There may be differences in the understanding and definition of certain concepts and terms in different languages and cultures. Failure to adequately consider language differences can lead to barriers to understanding and communication.

    这些风险表明，在进行定义的继承时，需要充分考虑时代背景、文化差异、语言差异等因素，及时更新和调整相关定义，保持定义的准确性和适用性，促进相关领域的发展。

    These risks indicate that when carrying out the succession of definitions, it is necessary to take into full consideration the background of the times, cultural differences, and language differences, to update and adjust the relevant definitions in a timely manner, to maintain the accuracy and applicability of the definitions, and to promote the development of the relevant fields.
