第2章 定义的基础

Chapter 2: The Foundation of Deﬁnitions

    定义的基础是语言和符号系统。语言是人类思维和交流的工具，而符号系统则是语言中用来表示特定意义的符号集合。通过语言和符号系统，人们能够将事物和概念进行抽象化和具体化，形成各种定义。因此，语言和符号系统是产生定义的根基。

    Definition is based on language and symbol systems. Language is a tool for human thinking and communication, while symbol systems are collections of symbols used in language to represent specific meanings. Through language and symbol systems, people can abstract and concretize things and concepts, forming various definitions. Therefore, language and symbol systems are the foundation for producing definitions.

    语言和符号系统的发展离不开人类的认知和经验。人类通过观察、实验、思考等方式逐渐认识事物和概念，并将其转化为语言和符号系统中的符号，形成各种定义。因此，定义的基础是人类的认知和经验，以及对语言和符号系统的运用能力。

    The development of language and symbol systems is inseparable from human cognition and experience. Humans gradually recognize things and concepts through observation, experimentation, and thinking, and transform them into symbols in language and symbol systems, forming various definitions. Therefore, the basis of definition is human cognition and experience, as well as the ability to use language and symbol systems.

    以下两个典型例子，进一步有助于理解语言和符号系统是产生定义的根基：

    The following two typical examples further help to understand that language and symbol systems are the foundation for producing definitions:

例1：数学中的符号系统

Example 1: Symbol System in Mathematics

    数学作为一门科学，具有自己独特的符号系统，如加减乘除等数学符号，以及各种函数、方程等。这些符号和符号系统是数学定义的基础，使得数学可以精确地描述和表达各种数学概念和理论。

    As a science, mathematics has its unique symbol system, such as mathematical symbols for addition, subtraction, multiplication, division, and various functions and equations. These symbols and symbol systems are the basis for mathematical definitions, enabling mathematics to accurately describe and express various mathematical concepts and theories.

    例如，数学中定义了“三角函数”的概念，即一个周期函数，描述了直角三角形中的角和边之间的关系。三角函数可以用各种符号表示，如sin(x)、cos(x)、tan(x)等，这些符号使得三角函数的定义可以被准确地传达和理解。

    For example, the concept of "trigonometric function" is defined in mathematics as a periodic function that describes the relationship between angles and sides in a right triangle. Trigonometric functions can be represented by various symbols, such as sin(x), cos(x), and tan(x), making it possible to accurately convey and understand the definition of trigonometric functions.

例2：自然语言的语言系统

Example 2: Language System in Natural Language

    自然语言是人类日常交流和沟通的工具，它由各种单词、语法规则和语言习惯组成。这些元素构成了自然语言的语言系统，为人们在日常生活中描述和表达各种事物和概念提供了基础。

    Natural language is a tool for human daily communication and exchange, consisting of various words, grammar rules, and language habits. These elements constitute the language system of natural language, providing a basis for people to describe and express various things and concepts in daily life.

    例如，在中文中，“水果”这一概念可以被定义为指“包括甜味的，可以生吃或制成甜品的植物果实或坚果”。这一定义使用了中文的语法规则和词汇，通过自然语言的符号系统精确地描述了“水果”的概念范围和特征。

    For example, in Chinese, the concept of "fruit" can be defined as referring to "sweet-tasting plant fruits or nuts that can be eaten raw or made into desserts." This definition uses Chinese grammar rules and vocabulary, and accurately describes the scope and characteristics of "fruit" through the symbol system of natural language.

    以下两个典型例子，进一步有助于理解定义的基础是人类的认知和经验：

    The following two typical examples further help to understand that the basis of definition is human cognition and experience:

例子1：定义“热水”

Example 1: Defining "Hot Water"

基于人类的感性认知和经验。

Based on human sensory cognition and experience.

    人类通过感觉器官来感知周围环境，将环境中的信息传递到大脑中进行处理。当我们用手触摸热水时，感知到的是热水的温度高于常温，同时还会感受到一定的热量。通过不断的感知和经验积累，我们可以形成对“热水”的认知。我们可以用语言或符号系统来表达和定义热水，例如，“热水”可以定义为水温高于42℃的水。

    Humans perceive the surrounding environment through sensory organs and process the information in the brain. When we touch hot water with our hands, we perceive that the temperature of the water is higher than normal and also feel some heat. Through continuous perception and experience accumulation, we can form a cognition of "hot water." We can use language or symbol systems to express and define hot water, for example, "hot water" can be defined as water with a temperature above 42℃.

例子2：定义“雨季”

Example 2: Defining "Rainy Season"

基于人类对季节变化的认知和经验。

Based on human cognition and experience of seasonal changes.

    人类在不断观察和感知周围环境中的变化，对季节变化有着深刻的认知和经验。在热带和亚热带地区，人们可以通过观察气象条件和自然景观变化来感知到雨季的来临。雨季的定义可以基于人类对气象和生态环境的认知和经验，例如，“雨季”可以定义为一个地区在连续的30天内出现5天或以上的降水量大于50毫米，或者根据当地生态环境的变化来进行定义。

    Humans have a profound cognition and experience of seasonal changes through continuous observation and perception of the surrounding environment. In tropical and subtropical regions, people can perceive the arrival of the rainy season through changes in weather conditions and natural landscapes. The definition of the rainy season can be based on human cognition and experience of meteorology and ecological environment, for example, "rainy season" can be defined as a region where there are 5 or more days of precipitation exceeding 50 millimeters in 30 consecutive days or according to changes in the local ecological environment.

    总之，定义的基础是人类的认知和经验，以及对语言和符号系统的运用能力。人类通过感官感知和思考，将所见所闻所思的对象抽象出来，并为其命名，构成了定义。语言和符号系统则为定义的传播和共享提供了必要的工具。通过定义，人们能够理清概念，归纳和分类事物，减少歧义，促进交流和协作，进而推动人类社会的进步。

    In summary, the basis of definition is human cognition and experience, as well as the ability to use language and symbol systems. Humans abstract the objects they see, hear, and think about through sensory perception and thinking, and name them, forming definitions. Language and symbol systems provide necessary tools for the dissemination and sharing of definitions. Through definitions, people can clarify concepts, induce and classify things, reduce ambiguity, promote communication and collaboration, and promote the progress of human society.
