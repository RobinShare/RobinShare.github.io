写作初衷

Original Intention of Writing

       面对人生意义的课题，随着人们知识的积累，生活基本温饱有了着落后，自然而然地，对自己生命存在的意义有所向往。对此，近些年及将来的很长一段时间，大多数的人们都将被这一课题困扰。甚至，这些人中的大多数，最后会发现自己的人生，根本不存在任何意义，妥妥地，工具人的一生。因此，撰写《定义学：探索概念的根源和意义》一书，其主要目的，正是为了探讨人生意义的本质，进而引导大多数的人们，重拾生命的火种，燃起心中的希望。

       When faced with the question of life's meaning, as people accumulate knowledge and secure their basic needs, they naturally yearn for a greater purpose in their existence. In the coming years and for a long time thereafter, the majority of people will grapple with this issue. Unfortunately, many will ultimately find their lives devoid of any true significance and feel like mere tools of their environment. Therefore, the main goal of writing the book, 'Definology: Exploring the Roots and Meanings of Concepts,' is to delve into the essence of life's meaning and inspire people to rekindle the flame of their spirit, igniting the hope within.

       《定义学：探索概念的根源和意义》的写作初衷是希望探讨人类对于概念和定义的认知方式，进而挖掘生命意义的本质。我们每个人都有着自己的信念和追求，但是这些信念和追求的背后，往往是我们对于生命意义的追逐。通过探讨概念的本质和意义，我们或许能够在生命的旅途中，找到更深层次的意义和方向，并燃起生命的火种。

       The original purpose behind writing 'Definology: Exploring the Roots and Meanings of Concepts' was to investigate how humans perceive and understand concepts and definitions, ultimately unearthing the essence of life's meaning. Each of us possesses our own beliefs and pursuits; however, these beliefs and pursuits are often driven by our pursuit of life's meaning. By delving into the true nature and significance of concepts, we may be able to discover more profound meaning and direction in the journey of life, ultimately igniting the flame of life within ourselves.

       本书的作者将认知科学、哲学等多个领域的思想融合在一起，凝聚成了一种全新的理解方式，以深刻的洞察力和严谨的思维模式，剖析了概念的起源和内涵，揭示了作为人类的我们，对于世界和自身的认知方式，同时对人生意义和存在的本质，进行了精辟的探讨。

       The author of this book combines ideas from multiple disciplines, including cognitive science, philosophy, and others, to form a new way of understanding. With profound insights and rigorous thinking, the book dissects the origin and connotation of concepts, revealing our cognitive means of understanding the world and ourselves as human beings. Additionally, the book provides insightful discussions on the essence of the meaning and existence of life, which are explored with great depth and precision.

       本书不仅仅是一本哲学的著作，更是一本让读者思考自己的内心深处的书。我们每个人都是独立的思考者，而《定义学：探索概念的根源和意义》的初衷在于激发我们对于生命意义的思考，引导我们去寻找生命的更深意义，让我们的生命变得更加意义丰富和充实。

       This book is not just a philosophical work but also a catalyst for encouraging readers to delve deeper into their inner selves. As independent thinkers, each of us has the potential for personal reflection and growth, and the ultimate goal of 'Definology: Exploring the Roots and Meanings of Concepts' is to inspire us to ponder the meaning of life and to guide us towards discovering deeper significance in our lives. By doing so, we can create a richer and more fulfilling existence.
